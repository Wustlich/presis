## 4. INFORMATION ON THE CONTENTS AND RESULTS OBTAINED

**4.1 Mode of Study:** Full-time

**4.2 Programme Requirements:**

The Bachelor's programme in Computer Science at TSU comprises **240 ECTS credits** distributed as follows:

| Component | ECTS | Share |
|---|---|---|
| Core Computer Science Courses | 130 | 54 % |
| Mathematics & Natural Sciences | 50 | 21 % |
| General Education & Languages | 30 | 13 % |
| Elective Courses | 15 | 6 % |
| Bachelor Thesis | 15 | 6 % |
| **Total** | **240** | **100 %** |

**4.3 Programme Details:**

The programme provides a comprehensive education in theoretical and applied computer science. Core topics include:

- **Programming:** Procedural programming (Python), Object-Oriented Programming (Java), Functional Programming
- **Computer Systems:** Operating Systems, Computer Architecture, Distributed Systems
- **Data & Algorithms:** Data Structures, Algorithms, Database Systems, Machine Learning Fundamentals
- **Networks & Security:** Computer Networks, Network Technologies & Security, Cloud Computing
- **Software Engineering:** Software Engineering, Software Architecture & Design Patterns, Web Programming
- **Mathematics:** Calculus, Linear Algebra, Discrete Mathematics, Probability & Statistics, Operations Research
- **Applied Topics:** Intelligent Systems / AI Basics, Mobile Application Development, Modelling & Simulation

**Accreditation:** The Computer Science programme at TSU holds **ABET accreditation** (Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology, USA) — the first ABET-accredited Computer Science programme in Georgia.

## 5. INFORMATION ON THE FUNCTION OF THE QUALIFICATION

**5.1 Access to Further Study:**
The qualification gives access to Master's programmes in Computer Science, Informatics, Software Engineering and related fields in Georgia and internationally.

**5.2 Professional Status:**
The qualification does not automatically confer the right to practise a regulated profession in Georgia. Graduates may work as software developers, systems analysts, data scientists, IT consultants and in related fields without further licensing requirements.
